About Us

At Music Tracker, we're passionate about bringing the magic of live music to your fingertips. Our platform offers a seamless way to dive into the vibrant world of music events, connecting you with concerts, and gigs in cities around the states. We are a small town bootcamp team looking to help the world get the best experience when it comes to music.

Our future developments:

Ticket Purchasing: Partner with ticketing platforms to enable users to purchase tickets directly through the Music Tracker website, streamlining the event discovery and booking process.

User Reviews and Ratings: Implement a feature that allows users to leave reviews and ratings for events they've attended, providing valuable feedback for other users and organizers.

Social Integration: Enable social media integration to allow users to share their favorite events, invite friends, and connect with fellow music enthusiasts.

Interactive Maps: Integrate interactive maps that allow users to visually explore music events in around the searched area, providing a dynamic and immersive browsing experience.
